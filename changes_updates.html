3.1  Brand Colour Alignment


BRAND COLOUR SPECIFICATION — PrivaLex Academy

The Academy palette must be complementary to PrivaLex Advisory (navy #1B2A6B + teal #00B9C7) but distinctly its own. Use the following palette:

Primary — Deep Teal: #006E7F (darker, more academic than Advisory teal)

Secondary — Warm Slate: #2C3E6B (a warmer navy that echoes Advisory without copying it)

Accent — Amber Gold: #F4A623 (warmth and achievement — appropriate for a certification brand)

Background — Off-White: #F8F9FC (clean, academic feel)

Text — Dark Charcoal: #1C2230

Relationship to Advisory: Advisory uses pure navy + cyan teal. Academy uses deep teal + warm navy + gold. The teal family connects them; the gold differentiates the Academy as a credentials and achievement brand.
